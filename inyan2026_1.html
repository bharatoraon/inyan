04 — About INYAN

WHAT IS
INYAN?

INYAN is the annual cultural and technical fest of the School of Planning & Architecture, Vijayawada — a two-day celebration that transforms the campus into a living laboratory of ideas, art, and human connection.

The name INYAN is rooted in the spirit of inquiry and expression — bringing together students, practitioners, and creatives across disciplines to explore what lies beyond the known. Each edition carries a theme that challenges the boundaries of design thinking. INYAN 2026 asks: what happens at the 65th square?

From immersive installations and workshops led by master craftspeople, to live performances, games, and a grand musical night — INYAN is where the campus comes alive.
